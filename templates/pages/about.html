About JamSplitter

JamSplitter is a powerful tool that uses AI to separate audio tracks into individual stems, such as vocals, drums, bass, and more. It's perfect for musicians, producers, and music enthusiasts who want to remix, study, or create new versions of their favorite songs.

Features

  • Separate audio into multiple stems (vocals, drums, bass, etc.)
  • Support for various audio formats (MP3, WAV, FLAC)
  • Fast processing with GPU acceleration
  • Simple and intuitive web interface
  • RESTful API for programmatic access

How It Works

  1. Enter a YouTube URL or upload an audio file
  2. Our AI processes the audio to separate the stems
  3. Download the separated tracks or listen to them directly in your browser
